Heap Space

  • Heap Space (2GB allocated to JVM). This 2GB willl divided by 2 part by configuration.
    • 1. Heap Area
    2. Non Heap Area
  • The Heap Area is divided into generations:
    • 1. The young generation stores short-lived objects that are created and immediately garbage collected.
    2. Objects that persist longer are moved to the old generation (also called the tneured generation).
  • GC will move survived objects in Eden Space and From Space to To Space. Then switch the role of To Space with From Space
